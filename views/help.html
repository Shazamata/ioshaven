Get help on installing .ipa files

Install an IPA File: Method 1

1. Make sure you have the latest version of iTunes installed on your computer.
2. Download the ipa file of your choice from this page.
3. Download Cydia Impactor using the links provided at the bottom of this page.
4. Extract the Impactor ZIP file and run the program labeled 'Impactor'.
5. Connect your iDevice to your computer.
NOTE: Make sure your iDevice is recognized by your computer.
6. Open Cydia Impactor and drag the .ipa file you downloaded into it.
7. Enter your Apple ID email into the field provided and click 'OK'.
8. Enter your Apple ID password into the field provided and click 'OK'.
NOTE: Your Apple ID is only used to generate a certificate from Apple and is not saved anywhere.
9. Wait for Cydia Impactor to sign the ipa.
NOTE: Wait to proceed until you see the 'Complete' message at the bottom of the program.
11. Unlock your device and go to Settings > General > Profiles and Device Management.
12. Open the profile with your Apple ID email on it and click 'Trust "your@email.com"'.
13. Go to your home screen and click on the app you downloaded in step 2.
14. You're done! Repeat this process every 7 days.

Install an IPA File: Method 2

1. Pay for and install the codesigning service of your choice.
NOTE: We recommend using builds.io.
2. Follow the instructions the codesigning company you chose provides.
3. That's it! Apps signed with a codesigning service never need to be re-signed.

Cydia Impactor Error Fixes

Fix the "provision.cpp:71" and "provision.cpp:81" errors.
Fix the "lockdown.cpp:57" error.
Fix the "provision.cpp:62", "provision.cpp:68", and "provision.cpp:150" errors.
Fix the "http-win.cpp:158" error.

Encounter an error that isn't on here? Do a quick Google search.
